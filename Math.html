Types/Method


Clifford Attractors: Millions of equation solutions are plotted as pixels on an x-y canvas. Coloring is added using photoshop.

Escape Trajectories: Using an iterative equation and a position in the complex plane as input, solutions are plotted as pixels in the complex plane. Pixels values end up representing the sum of all solutions that landed at that location. Only initial positions that increase exponentially throughout iterations are used, as they draw the most interesting shapes. Three plots with different maximum iterations are used as RGB channels to produce color images.
